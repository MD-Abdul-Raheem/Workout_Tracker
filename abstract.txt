IronLog AI is a high-performance Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed
to modernize resistance training tracking. Unlike traditional spreadsheets
or basic counters, IronLog acts as an intelligent training partner.

integrating Google's Gemini AI for workout generation. The application 
prioritizes speed, data privacy (Local-First Architecture), and a premium 
user experience via a sophisticated "Dark Glass" aesthetic.

    â€¢ Auto-Archival System:
      On startup, the app runs a background check comparing the current
